About the Artisan

My crafting journey began over 50 years ago with a simple crochet hook and a ball of yarn. What started as basic projects has blossomed into a lifelong passion, and I still get excited tackling new creative challenges today. Each piece reflects my commitment to quality, sustainability, and traditional craftsmanship techniques passed down through generations. I work from my home studio and take pride in creating unique, functional art that brings joy to everyday life.
